Sale Terms

1. TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP. The Seller hereby sells, transfers, and conveys to the Buyer the above-described motor vehicle for the stated purchase price, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged. This sale is final and paid in full.

2. TITLE STATUS. The Seller represents that the certificate of title for this vehicle is currently held by a third-party automobile dealership due to a separate and unrelated financial obligation of the Seller. The Seller affirms that the vehicle itself is free and clear of any liens, encumbrances, or security interests. The Seller agrees to cooperate fully with the Buyer in obtaining a replacement or bonded title through the Florida DHSMV, including but not limited to: signing any required affidavits, providing proof of ownership history, and appearing at the tax collector's office if necessary.

3. AS-IS SALE. This vehicle is sold "AS IS" with no warranties, express or implied, regarding its condition, merchantability, or fitness for any particular purpose. The Buyer has inspected the vehicle and accepts it in its present condition.

4. SELLER'S WARRANTY OF OWNERSHIP. The Seller warrants that they are the lawful owner of this vehicle and have the legal right to sell it. The Seller warrants that the vehicle is not stolen, and that there are no liens, claims, or encumbrances against the vehicle itself (distinct from the title situation described above).

5. INDEMNIFICATION. The Seller agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Buyer against any claims, damages, or expenses arising from any breach of the Seller's representations in this Bill of Sale.

6. GOVERNING LAW. This Bill of Sale shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida.
