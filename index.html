Frequently Asked Questions

When you create your account, your first 3 leads are completely free. This lets you see the lead quality and close real jobs before spending any money. After your 3 free leads, you add credit to your account to keep receiving leads.

Standard leads are $35 each after your 3 free leads. High-volume contractors ($500+ credit loads) qualify for $25/lead. You pre-load credit into your account starting at $100, and each lead is deducted from your balance. No monthly fees, no contracts, no surprises.

Leads are exclusive to your service area and vertical. You won't be competing with dozens of other contractors for the same customer. Be the first to call and close the job.

Every lead includes the customer's name, phone number, email address, service address, and details about the job they need done. Leads are delivered instantly via email, SMS, or both.

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can pause lead delivery from your dashboard at any time and resume when you're ready. Your credit balance stays on your account.

We stand behind our lead quality. If you receive a lead with invalid contact information or one that doesn't match your service area, contact us and we'll review it. Valid disputes are credited back to your account.

We generate leads for a variety of service industries including sod installation, mobile mechanic, landscaping, septic, welding, drainage, and more. During signup, you select the service types relevant to your business.
