Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

EzLead ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the EzLead platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

From Buyers (Service Contractors):

  • Name, business name, email address, phone number
  • Service area (zip codes), service type/vertical
  • Payment information (processed by our third-party payment processor)
  • Account activity: login history, lead delivery history, transaction history

From Leads (Potential Customers):

  • Name, phone number, email address, service address
  • Description of the service needed
  • This information is collected through our lead generation websites, phone calls, and online forms

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, time spent on site
  • Referral source

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Lead delivery: We share Lead contact information with Buyers in the matching service area and vertical so they can follow up on the service request.
  • Account management: To process payments, manage your account, and communicate with you about your service.
  • Service improvement: To improve lead quality, platform performance, and user experience.
  • Communication: To send account notifications, lead alerts, and service updates. We do not send marketing emails to Leads.

3. Information Sharing

We share information only as follows:

  • Lead information to Buyers: Lead contact details and service request information are shared with Buyers who service the Lead's area and vertical. This is the core function of our service.
  • Payment processors: Payment information is shared with our third-party payment processor to process transactions. We do not store full credit card numbers.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

We do not sell personal information to data brokers or third-party advertisers.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database storage, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain Buyer account information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. Lead information is retained for the purpose of service delivery and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses basic analytics to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or sell browsing data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered Buyers of material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at support@ezlead4u.com.
